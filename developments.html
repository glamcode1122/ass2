StokvelERA

Kickstarting a new era for stokvel and collective social capital

A revolutionary savings and investment platform designed specifically for our community network, including ZUKANO ASSI team members and stokvel groups. StokvelERA transforms traditional savings circles into powerful financial tools for community wealth creation.

Key Features

  • Digital stokvel management with automated contributions
  • Secure investment options with community-focused returns
  • Financial literacy and wealth-building education modules
  • Transparent governance and dispute resolution mechanisms
  • Integration with existing community financial practices

E-MAKAYA Platform

A digital community network for tribal communities

Introducing e-Makaya - a traditional communal shared-prosperity development strategy (Pilot 2050). This digital platform connects tribal communities, preserves cultural heritage, and facilitates economic collaboration while honoring traditional governance structures.

Key Features

  • Digital tribal governance and decision-making tools
  • Cultural heritage preservation through digital archives
  • Community resource sharing and collaborative projects
  • Inter-tribal communication and knowledge exchange
  • Traditional leadership integration with modern technology

Development Roadmap

Our strategic timeline for launching and scaling these innovative platforms

Q1 2024

Concept Development

Initial research and conceptualization of both StokvelERA and E-MAKAYA platforms, including community needs assessment and stakeholder engagement.

Q3 2024

Prototype Development

Creation of initial prototypes for both platforms, with focus group testing and iterative improvements based on community feedback.

Q1 2025

Pilot Launch - E-MAKAYA

Limited pilot launch of E-MAKAYA platform with 3 tribal communities to test functionality, usability, and cultural appropriateness.

Q3 2025

Full Launch - StokvelERA

Official launch of StokvelERA platform to ZUKANO ASSI network and partner stokvel groups, with full feature set and security protocols.

2026-2030

Expansion & Scaling

Gradual expansion of both platforms to wider communities, integration with financial institutions, and development of advanced features based on user feedback.

2030-2050

Vision Realization

Full realization of the E-MAKAYA 2050 vision, with comprehensive digital tribal networks established across Southern Africa and measurable improvements in community prosperity.
